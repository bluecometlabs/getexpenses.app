# Export Sheet as PDF

You can export your sheet as a PDF file. To do this, follow these steps on each platform:

- [iOS/iPadOS](#iosipados)
- [macOS](#macos)

---

## iOS/iPadOS

1. Open the sheet you want to export.
2. Tap the `Print` button.

    <img src="../../assets/faq/export-sheet-as-pdf/export-sheet-as-pdf-ios-1.jpg" width="40%">

3. Select `Save as PDF` button via the `Share` menu.

    <img src="../../assets/faq/export-sheet-as-pdf/export-sheet-as-pdf-ios-2.jpg" width="40%">

## macOS

1. Open the sheet you want to export.
2. Tap the `Print` button.

    <img src="../../assets/faq/export-sheet-as-pdf/export-sheet-as-pdf-mac-1.jpg" width="65%">

3. Select `Save as PDF` button via the `Share` menu.

    <img src="../../assets/faq/export-sheet-as-pdf/export-sheet-as-pdf-mac-2.jpg" width="65%">
