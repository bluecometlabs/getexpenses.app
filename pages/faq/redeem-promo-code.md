# Redeem Promo Code

Here is a simple step-by-step instruction of how to redeem your promo code. All you need is the promo code and your Apple ID with the password. For more information, please refer to [ï£¿ How to redeem your Apple Gift Card or App Store & iTunes gift card](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201209){:target="_blank"} from Apple Support.

- [iOS/iPadOS](#iosipados)
- [macOS](#macos)

---

## iOS/iPadOS

1. Open the App Store
2. Tap on your profile
3. Tap on `Redeem Gift Card or Code`
4. Enter the code manually or use Camera

## macOS

1. Open the App Store
2. Tap on your profile
3. Tap on `Redeem Gift Card`
4. Enter the code manually or use Camera
