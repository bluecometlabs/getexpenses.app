<h1>Sharing a sheet with others</h1>
<p>To share a sheet with others, people you added also need to unlock the full features because our Sharing uses iCloud sync. If the people still can't see anything you shared, please check the following on both sides (you and people you added).</p>

<p>On iOS,</p>
<ol>
    <li>Have enough storage space in iCloud on your device</li>
    <li>Go to Settings>Apple ID>iCloud and check the checkbox for Expenses app</li>
</ol>

<p>On macOS,</p>
<ol>
    <li>Have enough storage space in iCloud on your device</li>
    <li>Go to System Preferences>Apple ID>iCloud Drive(Options...) and check the checkbox for Expenses.app in Documents</li>
</ol>

<p>In Expenses app,</p>
<ol>
    <li>iCloud Sync is turned on in Settings>Sync</li>
    <li>In Settings>Sync, try force push all data to iCloud, and then try force fetch all data from iCloud</li>
</ol>
