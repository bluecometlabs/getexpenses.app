# Family Purchases

We have enabled Family Sharing functionality for the purchase. To access in-app purchases from an app purchased by another family member, the app should be downloaded from the family member's purchased history. For more information, please refer to [ï£¿ How to download apps and content that your family members purchased](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201085) from Apple Support.

1. Open the App Store
2. Tap on your profile
3. Select `Purchased`
4. Tap your family member in `Family Purchases`
5. Download the Expenses app from there
